About Me

Kaartikey Natrajan

velocitorq.com

I‘m an experienced software engineer and entrepreneur with skills in full-stack web, API and mobile development, machine learning, game development and 3D modelling.

I’m currently working as a Software Engineer Intern at Saltmine, while also pursuing my Master’s in Computer Science from the University of Massachusetts Amherst. I also hold a bachelor’s degree in Information Science and Engineering.

During my undergrad, I co-founded Estareal, an e-commerce platform for the sale of real estate construction items. As the sole technical member, I single-handedly built, deployed, and secured the entire software stack from scratch. Estareal, unfortunately did not make it to production because of certain business reasons but this failure prompted me to start my own venture, BlazeRacing (Velocitorq Games), an online, real-time, cross-platform, multiplayer racing game. As the founder and technical lead, I developed the entire application from the ground up, made all the business decisions related to the product and led a team of 3.

I also worked full time as a software engineer at Amadeus Labs, Bangalore where I functioned, primarily, as a UI engineer on SellConnect, the company’s flagship product. Additionally, I played the role of mentor for multiple interns for a 5-month period, participated in and won many technical events and hackathons and earned numerous team and division level awards.
